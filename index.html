About Unity Family Home LLC

Since our founding, we’ve helped families feel confident and supported with dependable, compassionate home care tailored to each person’s needs.

  • Compassion: We lead with kindness, respect, and patience, every visit.
  • Trust: Rigorous screening and ongoing training ensure reliable, professional care.
  • Safety: Evidence-based protocols and respectful support for peace of mind.

Our mission

To empower older adults to live with dignity and independence through compassionate, personalized care in the comfort of home.

Our vision

To be the most trusted home care provider in our community—known for exceptional caregivers, thoughtful support, and consistent outcomes.

Services

Flexible care plans that adapt to your family’s needs, delivered by skilled, compassionate professionals.

Companionship care

Meaningful conversation, games, walks, and social support to ease loneliness and enrich daily life.

Personal care assistance

Discreet support with bathing, grooming, dressing, and mobility to promote comfort and safety.

Skilled nursing

RN-oversighted services including medication management, wound care, vitals monitoring, and care coordination.

Meal preparation

Nutritional planning and home-cooked meals tailored to preferences, allergies, and physician guidance.

Transportation assistance

Safe, reliable rides to appointments, errands, and social activities with door-to-door support.
